Fine Arts Event: Sketching Contest
Date: 8 June 2026
Time: 11:00 AM – 2:00 PM
Venue: Fine Arts Studio
Maximum Participants: 60
Registration Deadline: 5 June 2026
About the Event:

The Sketching Contest encourages students to express ideas through pencil art and creative drawing. A theme will be provided at the beginning of the competition, and participants must complete their sketch within the given time.
Event Rules:

• Participants must bring their own sketching materials.
• Only pencil/charcoal sketches are allowed.
• Original artwork is mandatory.
• Judges’ decision will be final.
