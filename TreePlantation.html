Tree Plantation Drive
Date: 5 July 2026
Time: 8:00 AM – 11:00 AM
Venue: College Campus Garden Area
Organized By: NSS Unit
Maximum Participants: 150
Registration Deadline: 3 July 2026
About the Event:

The Tree Plantation Drive aims to promote environmental awareness and sustainability. Participants will plant saplings within the college campus and learn about the importance of trees in maintaining ecological balance and reducing pollution.
Event Rules:

• Wear comfortable clothes and shoes.
• Follow safety instructions.
• Handle plants carefully.
• Maintain discipline during the activity.
