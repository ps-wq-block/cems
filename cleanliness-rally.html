About the Event:

The Cleanliness Awareness Rally is organized to spread awareness about hygiene, waste management, and environmental cleanliness. Participants will carry banners and slogans to encourage the community to keep surroundings clean.
Event Rules:

• Wear comfortable clothes.
• Follow rally route guidelines.
• Maintain discipline during the rally.
• Avoid littering at any location.
